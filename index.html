JRK Electrical

Local Gisborne Electrical Company

Gisborne electrician JRK Electrical is a small, independently owned/operated electrical business servicing the electrical needs of Gisborne, Wairoa and the East Coast of New Zealand. The owner, Jose Ratahi-Koia, specialises in the service and electrical contracting sector. He can handle all aspects of the electrical trade, from adding lights and sockets in an existing home to a full commercial fit out, from re-wiring an older home to full installation in a brand new home. JRK Electrical provide a professional electrical contracting service and deliver quality at a competitive rate.
